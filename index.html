About me

I work on digital governance and data systems in public administration, with a particular focus on healthcare infrastructure and institutional interoperability. Currently based in Piedmont, I contribute to regional digital health initiatives and open-source projects. I believe the most interesting problems in public systems require understanding both the regulatory constraints and the technical possibilities.
